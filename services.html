Comprehensive Foot Care Services

Thorough Assessment

  • Complete evaluation of legs, feet, toenails, and lower extremities
  • Identification of complications or areas requiring attention
  • Personalized care plan based on your specific needs

Specialized Care

  • Diabetic foot care - Gentle, expert care for sensitive diabetic feet
  • Thick nail treatment - Management of nails affected by fungal infection or trauma
  • Corn and callus reduction - Safe removal for improved comfort and mobility
  • Ingrown toenail care - Professional treatment to relieve pain and prevent infection
  • Nail trimming and filing - Precise care for both toenails and fingernails
  • Wound assessment - Evaluation and recommendations for any foot wounds or skin concerns

Therapeutic Benefits

  • Foot and lower leg massage to improve circulation and reduce discomfort
  • Education on proper foot care techniques and prevention strategies
  • Professional referrals and recommendations when specialized care is needed
  • Product recommendations for at-home maintenance
