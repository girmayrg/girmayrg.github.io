Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to some of the most common questions about our mobile foot care services.



How long does a typical appointment take?

Most appointments last 45–60 minutes, allowing time for a thorough assessment and complete foot care treatment.



Do you accept insurance?

We provide invoices for reimbursement and can directly bill Alberta Blue Cross where applicable.



What should I prepare for my appointment?

Simply have a comfortable chair available. We bring all necessary equipment and supplies to your home.



How often should I schedule appointments?

Typically every 6–8 weeks, though frequency can be adjusted based on your individual needs and foot health.



Are you accepting new clients?

Yes! We welcome new clients and are happy to answer any additional questions you may have before booking.
